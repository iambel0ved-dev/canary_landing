Four steps.
Zero friction.

From URL to alert in under a minute. No code, no SDKs, no dashboards you have to learn.

Drop in any URL — a competitor's pricing page, an API endpoint, a product listing, a job board. If it's on the web, Canary can watch it.

Capabilities

Built for teams
who need to know first.

Intelligent Diff Engine+
Not just "page changed" — Canary shows you exactly what changed. Highlighted text diffs, JSON property changes, price old/new, DOM node insertions and deletions. Context that actually matters.
Check Intervals — Down to 30 Seconds+
Check any URL as frequently as every 30 seconds on Enterprise, 1 minute on Business, 5 minutes on Growth. Our distributed crawler network ensures checks run on time, every time — even for JavaScript-heavy SPAs rendered via headless Chrome.
AI Change Summaries+
Raw diffs are noisy. Canary uses Claude AI to summarize exactly what changed and why it matters — "Competitor added 'Enterprise SSO' to their pricing table" instead of 400 lines of HTML diff.
Full JavaScript Rendering+
A Playwright-powered browser fleet renders dynamic SPAs, React apps, and lazy-loaded content before diffing. No more missing changes hidden behind JavaScript.
Team Workspaces+
Shared monitors, role-based access, team notification routing, and audit logs. Marketing, engineering, and product can each see what matters to them — without the noise.
